What You'll Learn in Snack School

Science You Can
Actually Use.

Snack School is built around a single belief: understanding your biology makes better decisions easier. Every article starts with the science — the gut-brain axis, microbiome diversity, cortisol and stress signaling, satiety hormones — and then connects it to practical, everyday snacking choices.

We cover the connection between your gut microbiome and your brain — specifically how the 38 trillion microbes in your digestive system produce neurotransmitters, influence hunger hormones, and send signals through the vagus nerve that shape your mood, energy, and cravings. Most people have never been told this. It changes how you think about why you eat the way you do.

We also go deep on functional ingredients — ashwagandha, prebiotic chicory root fiber, amla fruit powder, monk fruit — explaining what they actually do at clinically relevant levels, rather than the trace-dose label decoration that most snack brands use. If you want to understand what's in your food and why it matters, this is where to start.
