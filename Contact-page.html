Before You Write

Quick Answers
to Common Questions

Where is my order? +
Orders typically ship within 1–2 business days. You'll get a tracking email as soon as yours is on its way. If it's been more than 3 days and you haven't heard anything, email us at hi@100CalSnacks.com and we'll sort it out immediately.
Can I change or cancel my order? +
If your order hasn't shipped yet, absolutely — reach out right away and we'll take care of it. Once it's shipped we can't redirect it, but we'll work with you on a solution.
Something arrived damaged — now what? +
We're sorry and we'll make it right, no questions asked. Email a photo to hi@100CalSnacks.com and we'll send a replacement or issue a refund — your choice.
How do subscriptions work? +
You can pause, skip, or cancel any time from your account — no hoops to jump through, no penalty. If you ever get stuck, just email us and we'll handle it in minutes.
Are you available for wholesale or retail? +
Yes — we'd love to talk. Select "Wholesale / Press" in the contact form above and tell us about your store or platform. We'll get back to you within one business day.
I just want to share my story. +
Please do. Genuinely — this brand was built from a personal story and hearing yours means everything. Select "My Story" in the form and write whatever you want to share. We read all of them.
