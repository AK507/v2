We Heard You.

We read every review — including the ones that sting a little. Here's some critical feedback we've received, and what we did with it. We show these because hiding them would be easier, and that's not how we do things.

★★☆☆☆ Critical Review
Too Dense for Me

"The taste is fine but the texture is heavier than I expected — more like a meal replacement than a snack. Not really what I was looking for."

— Verified Amazon Purchase, Jan 2026
Our Response

That's fair. Our bars are intentionally dense because the fiber and protein content is real — that's what makes them filling at 100 calories. They're not an airy snack bar. If texture is important to you, we'd recommend starting with the BBQ Protein Puffs, which have a lighter crunch. We'd rather you find the right fit than just reorder something that isn't working for you.

★★★☆☆ Critical Review
Shipping Was Slow

"Love the product, but my order took nearly two weeks to arrive. The snacks themselves are great — the logistics need work."

— Verified Amazon Purchase, Feb 2026
Our Response

This one hurt to read — because you're right, and we know it. We're a small team and logistics have been a real challenge at certain times. We've since switched fulfilment partners for faster dispatch. If your order ever arrives late, email us at hi@100calsnacks.com and we'll make it right. No questions asked.
