The 100Cal Story

Born from
the Hard
Stuff.

This brand didn't come from a boardroom or a business plan. It came from 5 surgeries, 50 extra pounds, a broken gut, and the kind of stubbornness that refuses to stay down.

Tapasya Bali
Founder & CEO, 100CalSnacks
01
Where It Started

I Didn't Start This
Because I Had It
Figured Out.

I started it because I was struggling — a well-dressed, overachieving, stress-eating mess, but struggling nonetheless.

For years I had the absolute pleasure of dealing with gut issues, anxiety, weight gain, and the kind of inflammation that makes you feel like your body is working against you. I was building a career, running a company, trying to keep it all together — and quietly falling apart on the inside.

Then came the surgeries. Five of them. And 50 extra pounds that showed up uninvited and refused to leave.

Setbacks don't break you.
They shape you.

I got the message. It was time for a change — not a dramatic reinvention, just a quiet, honest decision to start treating my body like it mattered.

02
The Problem

The Snacks Were
Either Joyless or
Gut-Wrecking.

I tried to clean up my diet. I read labels. I bought the "healthy" things. And I quickly discovered that most of them were either glorified cardboard, or they were full of sugar alcohols and additives that left my gut worse than before.

Erythritol. Maltitol. Artificial sweeteners. Ingredients I couldn't pronounce, in amounts that were clearly there for cost savings, not for me.

The protein bars that gave me bloating. The "clean" snacks that tasted like despair. The options that were technically healthy but made me feel like I was being punished for wanting to eat better.

Why should eating
well feel like
giving something up?

That question sat with me for a long time. And eventually, it turned into a decision.

The Moment Everything Changed

So I Made
My Own.

100CalSnacks was born from a simple idea: snacking should nourish you, not punish you. That every calorie should earn its place. That you can have something that tastes like a real treat and works like a functional supplement — at the same time.

I went deep on the science. Gut-brain axis. Prebiotic fiber. Adaptogens. Ashwagandha. The connection between what you eat and how you think, feel, and handle stress. And I built snacks around that science instead of around margins and marketing.

No sugar alcohols. No palm oil. No artificial anything. Zero compromise on what goes inside — and zero compromise on taste.

A Note from the Founder

If you've read this far — thank you. Genuinely.

Building 100CalSnacks has been the hardest and most rewarding thing I've ever done. There were moments I wasn't sure we'd make it. Moments where the easier path would have been to cut corners, use cheaper ingredients, make the snacks taste a little less real in exchange for a better margin.

I never did. And I won't.

Because this brand isn't a business to me first — it's a commitment. To everyone who's ever stood in a grocery store aisle feeling like there's nothing there for them. To everyone managing stress and gut health and trying to figure out how to eat well without it feeling like punishment. To everyone who just wants a snack they can trust.

You deserve better. We're here to give it to you.

And if you ever want to talk — about the products, about the science, about your own journey — my door is open. That's not a marketing line. That's just how we do things here.

Tapasya Bali
